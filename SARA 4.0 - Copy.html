✨ All Features
What can SARA do for you?
SARA is your Smart Accessible Reader & Assistant — built especially for visually impaired K–12 students. Every single feature is designed to work with audio, keyboard, and voice.
🔊 Audio First
🎙️ Voice Controlled
⌨️ Keyboard Accessible
🤖 Gemini AI Powered
🎙️
Voice Interaction Engine
CORE FEATURE
🔊 Text-to-Speech — every response read aloud
🎙️ Voice commands — say "Start Quiz", "Read lesson"
⏸️ Pause, rewind, and re-read any response
🐢 Adjustable speech speed — slow for younger kids
🔤 Phonetic mode — spells out difficult words
🤖
AI Subject Assistant
GEMINI POWERED
📚 Covers Math, Science, English, History, Geography
🎓 Grade-aware — adjusts language for K–12 levels
🧩 Explains concepts using feel-based analogies
🚫 Never uses visual references ("look at this…")
🔁 Ask follow-up questions in natural language
📝
Quiz & Exam Preparation
EXAM READY
🔊 Questions & all options read aloud one by one
⌨️ Answer with A/B/C/D keys — no mouse needed
⏱️ Timed Exam Mode with verbal countdowns
💬 Instant explanation after every answer
📊 Weak topic auto-detection & revision
🃏
Audio Flashcards
MEMORISATION
🔊 Reads question, pauses, then reads answer
⬅️➡️ Arrow keys to go forward and backward
↩️ Press Enter to flip card and hear the answer
🤖 AI-generated card sets for any topic
🔁 Spaced repetition — re-shows forgotten cards
📄
PDF & Textbook Reader
READING AID
📖 Reads uploaded textbook PDFs page by page
🖼️ OCR — converts scanned book images to audio
✂️ Smart summary: "Give me a 2-minute summary"
📊 Describes tables & charts in plain words
⠃ Braille-ready text output for display devices
🔢
Math Accessibility Engine
STEM SUPPORT
📢 Reads equations aloud: "x squared plus 2x"
🪜 Step-by-step verbal problem solving
📐 Geometry shapes described without visuals
📈 Graph narration — "upward sloping line at 0,2"
🧮 Voice calculator — say a sum, hear the answer
🌐
Multi-Language Support
8 LANGUAGES
🇮🇳 Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi & more
🔁 Bilingual mode — English + native language
🗣️ Full TTS in all supported languages
🔤 Auto language detection from student input
⚙️ Change language any time from Settings
🏆
Gamification & Rewards
MOTIVATION
⭐ Points earned for every lesson & quiz
🔥 Daily study streak tracker with voice praise
🏅 Achievements announced with celebration sound
🎯 Leaderboard position read aloud
🎮 Story-Quest mode — learning as an adventure
📊
Progress Tracking & Smart Review
PERSONALISED LEARNING
📈 Audio progress report — spoken summary of performance
